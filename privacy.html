Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

GoalBond ("we", "us", or "our") operates the GoalBond mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Contact us at: privacy@goalbond.app

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and authentication credentials managed securely by Supabase Auth
  • Profile information: display name, profile photo, workout type, fitness level
  • Check-in photos: live photos taken within the app for daily check-ins
  • Usage data: streaks, partnerships, check-in history
  • Device information: device type, operating system, app version
  • Communications: messages sent between partners within the app

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the GoalBond service
  • To facilitate partnerships and streak tracking between users
  • To send push notifications about check-in status and streaks
  • To display your profile to potential partners
  • To improve and optimize the app

4. Photo Storage

Check-in photos are stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure. Photos are only visible to you and your active partner.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — database and storage infrastructure
  • Firebase — push notification delivery
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — app distribution and billing
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment processing

6. Automated Processing

Our backend infrastructure uses automated processing to deliver app features including streak calculations, window resets, and notification triggers.

7. Your Rights (PIPEDA — Canada)

Under Canadian privacy law you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights contact: privacy@goalbond.app

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, most data is deleted immediately. Some backup copies may be retained for a limited period before automatic permanent deletion.

9. Children's Privacy

GoalBond is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@goalbond.app and we will delete it immediately.

10. Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure is hosted on Supabase.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@goalbond.app
